Why More Seniors Will Start Spending Their Final Days at Home

by Healthcare Practice Leaders | July 11, 2018

In response to the growth of the aging population, healthcare providers are planning to focus near-term investment in home health care and models that support it. For these innovative models to be successful, patients and their providers will need to speak openly about what matters most to patients in their final days.
